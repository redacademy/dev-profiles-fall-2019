Best Travel Experience

As a graduation present to myself after my BA, I took a VIARail train ride from Vancouver to Toronto. The Rockies were majestic as one would and will always be told to expect, but what won me over was the night sky over the prairies, the ever expanding, all consuming darkness dotted with bright stars. It was here that I finally learned what it means to be breathlessly in awe.
